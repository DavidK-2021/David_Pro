Appearance.Super Sonic appears the same as he does in "Sonic the Comic" He has the same appearence as Classic Super Sonic, with yellow fur and spiked up hair. The only notable difference is the eyes, which appear as red and white spirals instead of the usual green irises, and the toothy grin, which usually isn't so jagged.Unlike Super Sonic in the mainstream Sonic Franchise, who maintains his heroic and cheerful attitude when he's not empowered, Super is the complete opposite, as he is dark, reckless and blood hungry. He is also very sadistic, as when he thought he killed Sonic's friends, he lets out a horrifying bowl of laughter. After suffering from amnesia, Super acts just as kind and nice as his non-super counterpart. Even when he regains his memories, he refuses to use his powers out of fear that he'll return to being the heartless monster he once was.

TriviaMany of his death quotes refer to Sonic.exe mockingly. For instance: "If little blue hedgehogs covered in blood scare you, you ain't ready to fight me." Furthermore, Sonic saying "NOW I’LL SHOW YOU!" might be a reference to Super Smash Bros. Brawl, in which his Final Smash, he turns into Super Sonic and says "NOW I'LL SHOW YOU!" and grunts.
